Our Story

In a small city Mysuru, where ancient palace lay,
A pair of star-crossed lovers met one day.

Dashing Deekshith & Vibrant Vidhyashree,
Two hearts entwined in love they couldn't flee

After denying the obvious for far too long,
They've found each other, where they truly belong.

The thought of things working out fills me with glee.
They now declare that they are together, each other's spouse to be

Finally, they confess, their love for all to see,
A union so sweet, like a symphony in harmony.

Their hearts, once guarded, now beat as one,
With a bond so strong, it cannot be undone.

The world may judge, but they don't understand,
The depth of love that they have at hand.

For the thought of things working out fills them with joy,
A lifetime of love, nothing can destroy.

As they take their vows and exchange their rings,
Their love story begins, like the start of spring.

And though life may bring its ups and downs,
Together they stand, through smiles and frowns.

So here's to their love, may it forever bloom,
A beautiful journey, like the sweetest perfume.
